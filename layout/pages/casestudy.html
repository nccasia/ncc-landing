CaseStudy

With intensive knowledge and professional experience in a variety of technologies, our outsourcing services can cover the entire software development life cycle and meet business needs in variety of industries

our process

We apply agile/scrum methodology to bring the most updated and optimized product

Requirements analysis

Requirement analysis is the most important and fundamental stage in SDLC. This information is then used to plan the basic project approach and to conduct product feasibility study in the economical, operational and technical areas.

Design and Architecture

This phase comes out with the best architecture for the product to be developed. Based on the requirements specified in the previous phase, usually more than one design approach for the product architecture is proposed and documented in a DDS

Development

After all architecture and design choices are already defined, the actual coding starts. Quality assurance and software testing

In this phase, the developed software is tested thoroughly and any defects found are assigned to developers to get them fixed.

Deployment

After the software has been tested, the final deployment takes place. But, this is not as simple as the click of a button. There are a lot of things to consider in this software development life cycle process.

Review

In the final phase of the SDLC, itâ€™s important to acknowledge that all software requires review. If users find any issues, the problem can be fixed in the next release

Finance & Insurance

ESG

Clients A dynamic and innovative, technology-focused Singaporean provider of collection, management and disclosure of material and actionable data solutions to businesses.
Client Needs A network for events planner and attendance to connect and exchange. It is relied on NFC readers and blockchains. The network strives to be among the top solutions for resolving the problem of mistrust between event stakeholders and offering the ecosystem necessary to produce self-organizing programmable events.
Our solutions NCC create a team of 9 people in which 6 are devs and 2 are QAs. We came up with the solution of using tickets which is unique to each event for atendance to use and exchange for drinks, food, and others in the event. Every purchase is contactless which enhance the trust between stakeholders and event organizer. We built 2 version of the network: One use Stellar and the other use Polygon. We also utilized React - native NodeJS and Microservice.

Research and analysis

Clients Global consulting firm that partners with leaders in business and society to tackle their most important challenges and capture their greatest opportunities.
Client Needs A platform for users to conduct their own research to identify and compare industry players, trace funding transactions and other connections among companies & analyze market trends. The main components are: Atlas Search Navigator for classifying fintechs across technology, business, and product lines and drilling down into precise subcategories; Smart Visualize for creating dynamic maps, trend charts, and other data visualizations; and the Fintech Profile Pages for diving deep into startupsâ€™ partnerships, use cases, and financials.
Our solutions Base on the professional experience on fintech industry, NCC formed a team of 20 skilled members including PM, Technical Leader, BA, Developers, QA, DevOps. The tickets in project were devided into two main flows, the first one for ETL sub-team and the second one for backend& frontend. The ETL sub-team was in charged of checking and finding fintech companies that have not been added to DB via client source and external source via AWS. The backend & frontend focused on displaying the fintech companies that have not been added to internal database. Testers in the ETL folow needed to query data in DB to compare with data API returned to each company. In the Frontend& Backend flow, they had to double check the features sharing functions such as realtime company, filter in other features to assure that there is no mistake with the current data flow. The main issue in most of tasks is depending on data from external API so if there was a wrong external data the team would have to detech and check all of data flow of ETL and Frontend & Backend to figure out the mistake. To handle this challenge, the team is active in implementing, creating test case and follow data in advance so that can take time and have plan to solve the arising problem but not affect the progress of project. With this professional and effective working manner, NCC has become the trusted partner of client for 3 years.

DLT-based trade finance platform
