Meet Awesome Team

Nhan Nguyen Tran

Chief Executive Officer

Graduated from Lomonosov Moscow State University with a bachelor's degree in Physics, but then Nhan discovered his passion in software development and he decided to learn to be a developer. Nhan has more than 15 years of working experience in software development and most of that time working with JAVA, C/C++, and mobile technologies (IOS, Android) and has gained a certain professionalism level as a Senior JAVA, C/C++ LINUX/MOBILE and cloud platform.

Tien Pham Manh

Deputy Chief Executive Office

Tien Pham is a software engineer with a strong background in project management and IT process management. With more than 10 years of experience, Tien has shown his potential in the position of technical leader. Tien has great knowledge of computers, network hardware, office facilities, especially Project Management Tools, blockchain, Ethereum network, and smart contracts.

Phuc Duong Van

Deputy Chief Executive Office

Phuc Duong has a Master’s degree in Computer Science and used to be a lecturer at a leading technology training university in Vietnam. Phuc has more than 10 years of experience in many specialties, such as .Net Framework, game development and mobile platform. With a problem - solving mindset, Phuc has become technical leader, project manager of many projects and received lots of appreciation from clients.

Linh Nguyen Le

Chief Human Resource Officer

team values

Lean Engineering Environment

Progressiveness

The wave of technology keeps moving, stopping means failure. NCC will maintain an active status at all times, adapting to market and demand fluctuations. NCC employees are always looking for ways to improve the outcomes and to better our profession so that we can handle heavy workloads and produce excellent results.

Pride of ownership

NCC encourages its employees to work with zeal, passion, and joy, with the goal of providing the best possible experience for our customers. We are proud of what we get and what we do. It does not mean that we are complacent about our work, it means that we respect every effort to achieve these successes. It is our motivation to deal with challenges and difficulties, and go further in our journey.

Collaborative attitude

We understand that solidarity is one of the most important factors which determine the success of any organization. Since the extremely rapid and powerful development of technology, it is not possible for an individual to keep up and cover all the movements and improvements, therefore, to provide diverse and comprehensive services, NCC always strives to strengthen the spirit of solidarity and cooperative attitude of all members in the company.
